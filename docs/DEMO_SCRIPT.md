# Five-Minute Demo Script

1. Open the landing page and click `Enter Demo Dashboard`.
2. Point out total job revenue, job costs, gross profit, cash collected, review queue, low-margin jobs, and recent uploads.
3. Go to Upload, download the sample CSV, upload it, review mapped columns and income/expense totals, then import.
4. Go to Transactions, filter or search, edit one category, assign one transaction to a job, and mark it reviewed.
5. Open the assigned job detail and show costs, cash collected, gross profit, margin, and expense breakdown.
6. Open Reports and show profitability by job, spending by category, monthly income versus expenses, unassigned expenses, low-margin jobs, and cash collected versus revenue.
7. Download the QuickBooks-ready CSV and the job-profitability CSV.
8. Open Settings, explain QuickBooks sandbox is optional, then reset the demo with `DEMO_RESET_TOKEN`.
